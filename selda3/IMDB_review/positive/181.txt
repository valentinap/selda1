this movie was really well done 100% through. it is one of those films that is a classic because it is a powerful story but its also an intelligent film. we dont see that too much and i hope to see more films like this it makes me think of the essence of filmmaking and storytelling and how much that aspect of our industry is really honored. every director wants to tell a great story but few let the story tell itself. this is an intelligent film because it gets so involved with our character driven plot lines that you forget youre watching a movie at all. as a young filmmaker this film is a fantastic model to build a foundation around. lets just hope studio thoughts dont pick up and they try to make a sequel/prequel...
